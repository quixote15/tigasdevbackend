## Post-Processing Notes
- After generation, manually clean up to exact 64x64 pixel grid
- Ensure thick black outlines are consistent (2px minimum, uneven/organic)
- Verify transparent background is clean (no artifacts around edges)
- Adjust colors to match palette hex codes exactly
- Add intentional asymmetry if AI output is too "perfect" or "clean"
- Dirty up clean surfaces with 1-2 noise pixels in shadow color
- The SMILE must be consistent across all non-death frames -- copy/paste mouth if needed
- Assemble frames left-to-right in horizontal sprite sheet per animation
- Test at 8-12fps to verify the "jerky" animation feel
- The nervous blink frames should feel SUDDEN, not smooth-transitioning
