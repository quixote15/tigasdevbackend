# ENEAS CARNEIRO (O Fantasma Patriota) -- Prompts para Geracao de Arte IA

## Diretrizes Gerais de Tom

> **ATENCAO**: Os prompts para Eneas sao FUNDAMENTALMENTE diferentes dos demais personagens.
> Enquanto outros personagens sao satiricos, grotescos e debochados, Eneas e tratado com
> REVERENCIA EPICA. A comedia nao vem de zoar o Eneas -- vem do ABSURDO de tanta reverencia
> num jogo de humor. Eneas e sagrado. O humor e meta: "por que esse fantasma e tratado como
> um deus aqui?" -- ESSA e a piada.

### Palavras-chave OBRIGATORIAS em todo prompt de Eneas:
- `reverent`, `dignified`, `sacred`, `ghostly`, `translucent`, `holographic`
- `iconic thick mustache`, `white suit`, `golden aura`, `Brazilian flag cape`
- `underground comix style`, `Robert Crumb`, `thick irregular outlines`

### Palavras-chave PROIBIDAS em prompts de Eneas:
- `funny`, `silly`, `goofy`, `ridiculous` (a comedia e INVOLUNTARIA)
- `zombie`, `undead`, `decayed` (Eneas NAO e zumbi -- e fantasma PURO)
- `cute`, `chibi`, `kawaii` (Eneas e EPICO, nao fofo)

## PROMPT 01: Sprite Sheet Base -- Idle + Walk

### Prompt Principal (Midjourney/DALL-E/Stable Diffusion)
```
pixel art sprite sheet, 64x64 pixels per frame, 4 frames idle animation,
underground comix art style inspired by Robert Crumb and Brazilian political cartoons,

character: dignified translucent ghost of a Brazilian politician from the 1990s,
MASSIVE iconic thick handlebar mustache that is the most prominent visual feature,
the mustache is slightly more opaque than the rest of the ghostly body,
wearing a glowing white suit with lapels, military-straight posture,
Brazilian flag flowing as a cape behind him,
golden patriotic aura surrounding the entire figure,

holographic translucent effect: the character is semi-transparent like a Star Wars hologram,
horizontal scanlines visible across the body at 3px intervals,
golden particles floating upward around the figure,
edge glow along the body outline in soft white,
the character floats 2-3 pixels above ground,

color palette: ghostly white (#E8E0D0) at 65% opacity for suit,
dark brown (#3A2A1A) at 80% opacity for mustache (MOST OPAQUE element),
golden (#D4A017 #FFD700) for aura and particles,
Brazilian flag colors at 50% opacity for cape,

proportions: caricature style, head 1.5x normal, stocky body approximately 5 heads tall,
thick irregular outlines 1-2px (cleaner than typical Crumb but still hand-drawn feel),
cross-hatching shadows, paper texture overlay,

background: transparent (alpha channel),
the overall feeling should be EPIC and REVERENTIAL, not comedic,
this is a legendary figure treated with genuine affection and sacred respect,
sprite sheet horizontal strip layout
```

### Prompt de Refinamento (img2img)
```
refine this pixel art sprite to add:
- holographic scanline effect (horizontal lines every 3 pixels, white at 8% opacity)
- pulsating golden aura that extends 4-6 pixels beyond the body outline
- the mustache should have its own subtle animation: tips slightly moving up and down
- afterimage trail effect: a faded copy of the sprite 2 pixels behind at 20% opacity
- floating golden particles (1-2px dots) rising slowly around the figure
- the Brazilian flag cape should have cloth physics: billowing gently to one side
- maintain the SACRED and DIGNIFIED tone -- this is not a joke character
```

## Guia de Uso dos Prompts

### Para Midjourney
- Adicionar: `--ar 1:1 --s 250 --q 2 --v 6`
- Para sprite sheets: `--ar 4:1` (idle 4 frames) ou `--ar 6:1` (walk 6 frames)
- Usar `--no gradient, smooth shading, 3D, realistic` para manter estilo pixel art

### Para DALL-E 3
- Adicionar ao inicio: `Create a pixel art image. `
- Ser mais explicito com dimensoes: `The image should be exactly [X]x[Y] pixels.`
- DALL-E tende a suavizar -- adicionar: `Use hard pixel edges, no anti-aliasing, no smooth gradients.`

### Para Stable Diffusion (Automatic1111 / ComfyUI)
- Modelo recomendado: `pixel-art-xl` ou `retro-pixel-art`
- Negative prompt: `smooth, gradient, 3D, realistic, blurry, antialiased, soft edges, modern`
- Sampler: `DPM++ 2M Karras`, Steps: 30-40, CFG: 7-9
- Para sprite sheets: usar ControlNet com template de grid

### Para Aseprite (Manual com Referencia IA)
- Gerar conceito com IA primeiro, depois redesenhar manualmente em Aseprite
- RECOMENDADO: Este e o metodo que melhor respeita o estilo Andre Guedes
- A IA gera a IDEIA, o artista executa com as nuances certas

## Checklist de Qualidade

Antes de aprovar qualquer asset do Eneas, verificar:

- [ ] O bigode e o elemento MAIS OPACO e MAIS PROEMINENTE?
- [ ] O efeito holograma/translucido esta presente?
- [ ] Scanlines horizontais visiveis?
- [ ] Aura dourada ao redor do corpo?
- [ ] Particulas douradas flutuando?
- [ ] Postura ERETA e DIGNA (nao curvada ou grotesca)?
- [ ] Outlines mais limpos que outros personagens (1-2px vs 2-4px)?
- [ ] Bandeira do Brasil como capa visivel?
- [ ] O tom geral e de REVERENCIA, nao de comedia?
- [ ] Cross-hatching nas sombras?
- [ ] Textura de papel sutil?
- [ ] Alpha channel correto (transparencia)?
