## INSTRUCAO GLOBAL DE ESTILO (prefixo para TODOS os prompts)

Copie este bloco no inicio de cada prompt de geracao:

```
Brazilian underground comix style, grotesque political caricature, thick black outlines (2-3px),
heavy cross-hatching shadows, dirty saturated color palette, Robert Crumb meets Brazilian
political humor meets B-movie horror. Deliberately ugly and exaggerated proportions.
Pixel art, 64x64 sprite, transparent background, top-down slightly isometric perspective.
NO smooth lines, NO clean art, NO anime style. UGLY, DEFORMED, FUNNY.
```

## PERSONAGEM BASE -- Referencia de Consistencia

### Prompt Master (usar como referencia para manter consistencia entre sprites)

```
Pixel art character sprite, 64x64px, transparent background.

CABO DACIOLO, Brazilian politician prophet character. Grotesque caricature style,
thick black outlines, underground comix aesthetic.

ANATOMY: Tall and lanky/skinny body, ENORMOUS bulging eyes (3x normal size, bloodshot,
pupils tiny, whites dominating), OVERSIZED mouth perpetually open as if about to scream
"GLORIA A DEUS!", prominent jawline, gaunt cheekbones. HUGE hands (2x normal, holding
giant Bible). Long thin neck.

CLOTHING: Pristine white dress shirt (MIRACULOUSLY clean despite zombie apocalypse),
sleeves rolled to elbows, two buttons open at chest. Black/dark gray dress pants with
sharp creases. Black dress shoes, worn but intact. NO tie (man of the people). Brown
rosary with golden crucifix hanging from neck.

ACCESSORIES: GIANT BIBLE (almost torso-sized, brown-gold leather cover, yellowed pages,
worn corners, HEAVY). CROOKED HALO floating above head (tilted 15 degrees, golden glow,
pulsing). HOLY SMOKE permanently emanating from hands (golden-white wisps).

EXPRESSION: Permanent state of religious ecstasy. Wide-eyed divine illumination. Mouth
ready to shout. The face of a man who KNOWS God is real because he's SEEN it.

COLOR PALETTE: Pure white (#FFFFFF) shirt, golden (#FFD700) halo, celestial blue (#87CEEB)
divine aura, yellow (#FFFF00) light rays, warm skin tone (#D4956A), dark outlines (#1A1A1A).

Style reference: Andre Guedes cartoon (Brazilian political caricaturist), Robert Crumb
underground comix, Fleischer Brothers rubber hose mixed with pixel art grotesque.
```

## NOTAS FINAIS PARA ARTISTAS

1. **Consistencia**: O Daciolo deve ser RECONHECIVEL em TODOS os frames -- olhos enormes, boca aberta, camisa branca, aureola torta. Mesmo em silhueta, o perfil deve ser inconfundivel.

2. **Exagero progressivo**: Quanto mais poderosa a skill, mais GROTESCAS as deformacoes anatomicas. Idle = 120% exagero. Fumaca Santa = 300% exagero.

3. **Fumaca nas maos NUNCA para**: Mesmo nos frames onde nao e mencionada, deve haver ao menos 1-2 wisps. E a marca registrada.

4. **Aureola NUNCA desaparece**: Exceto durante o frame de vergonha do Erro Comico (1 frame).

5. **A Biblia tem PESO**: Toda animacao deve mostrar que a Biblia e PESADA. Bracos se esforçam, corpo compensa, balance afetado.

6. **Andre Guedes, nao Disney**: Linhas GROSSAS, sombras PESADAS, cores SUJAS, formas DISTORCIDAS. Se parecer bonito, esta ERRADO.

7. **Ele e o CORACAO do jogo**: Os assets do Daciolo devem ser os MAIS detalhados, os MAIS expressivos, os MAIS polidos de todos os personagens. Ele merece.
