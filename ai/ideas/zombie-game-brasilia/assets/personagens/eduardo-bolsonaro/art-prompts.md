## Post-Processing Notes
- After AI generation, manually clean up to exact pixel grid (64x64 or 32x32)
- Ensure thick black outlines are consistent (2-3px minimum for character, 2px for projectile)
- Verify transparent background is clean (no artifacts, no halo)
- Adjust colors to match palette hex codes exactly from sprite-spec.md
- Add intentional asymmetry if AI output is too "perfect" or "clean"
- Dirty up clean surfaces with 1-2 noise pixels in shadow colors
- The MOON FACE must be the dominant visual feature in every single frame
- If the chin appears in any generated image: PAINT OVER IT. Remove it. No chin allowed
- The eyes must look WET and SAD in every frame -- add tear pixels if AI forgot
- Assemble frames left-to-right in horizontal sprite sheets
- Test animations at target FPS before finalizing -- the "jerkiness" is intentional
- Color-shift skin slightly toward yellow to match the gross Crumb aesthetic
- Ensure suitcase is present in EVERY frame except post-death ghost
