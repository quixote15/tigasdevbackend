# Janja (A Verdadeira Presidente) - Art Prompts

Estilo visual: Andre Guedes -- caricaturas grotescas, underground comix, Robert Crumb + humor politico brasileiro + horror B-movie. Cores saturadas-sujas, exagero proposital, linhas grossas, sombras pesadas.

---

## PROMPT MESTRE (usar como prefixo em TODOS os prompts)

```
Underground comix style political caricature, grotesque exaggerated anatomy, thick black ink outlines, heavy cross-hatching shadows, Robert Crumb meets Brazilian political satire meets B-movie horror. Dirty saturated color palette. Top-down slightly isometric perspective for 2D game sprite. 64x64 pixel art reference. NO smooth lines, NO clean anime style -- everything must look hand-drawn, rough, twitchy, deliberately ugly-beautiful.
```

## 1. CHARACTER SHEET / REFERENCIA PRINCIPAL

```
CHARACTER SHEET -- "JANJA, The True President"

Underground comix grotesque caricature of a Brazilian political wife who secretly runs the government. Standing power pose, front/side/back views.

HAIR: Absurdly oversized blonde helmet-hair, disproportionately HUGE compared to her head, like a golden football helmet made of hair. Stiff, shiny, moves as one solid mass. Golden highlights. The hair IS the joke -- it's bigger than her entire head.

FACE: Enormous mouth with thick red lipstick -- mouth takes up 75% of the face. She talks more than everyone. Eyes are calculating, sharp, always scheming. Heavy eye makeup, slightly smeared.

BODY: Tight black designer dress covered in subtle luxury brand logos. Thin arms ending in long claw-like red fingernails (like talons gripping a corporate credit card). High heels exaggerated to 4x normal height.

ACCESSORIES: A handbag BIGGER than her entire torso (grotesquely oversized designer bag, brown leather with gold buckle). Corporate credit card in one claw-hand. Cellphone in the other. Giant gold earrings that swing independently.

STANDING ON: A red carpet that unrolls ahead of her and disintegrates into flying money bills behind her.

Color palette: golden blonde (#DAA520, #FFD700), black (#0D0D0D), blood red (#8B0000), money green (#228B22), gold (#FFD700), red lipstick (#CC0033). Heavy black outlines. Dirty saturated colors.

Style: Robert Crumb underground comix, grotesque political caricature, Brazilian satirical art, B-movie horror aesthetic.
```

## 7. SPECIAL -- FORA ELON MUSK!

```
Special attack animation, 6 frames, sprite sheet. Grotesque political caricature woman performing a SONIC SCREAM that disables all technology.

FRAME 1-2 (BUILDUP): Hair GROWS upward (+50% size), standing up like static electricity. Face turns deep red. She drops her own cellphone (ironic). Fists clenched. Yellow electricity particles surround her.

FRAME 3 (THE SCREAM): Mouth opens to 90% OF HER FACE (maximum grotesque deformation). Concentric sonic waves blast outward from her mouth (orange-red arcs). Text projectile "FORA ELON!" flies forward in bold red. Hair at maximum electrified volume. Entire sprite shakes.

FRAME 4-5 (EMP WAVE): Blue electric pulse circle expands outward from her. Electronic devices nearby spark and die. Electric particles in all directions. Screen shows static interference.

FRAME 6 (RECOVERY): Mouth closes into satisfied smile. Hair slowly deflates back to normal. Picks up cellphone. Victory pose, hand on hip.

Based on real G20 incident. Underground comix style, Robert Crumb + Brazilian political satire. Maximum grotesque energy.
```

## NOTAS PARA O ARTISTA

1. **O cabelo e TUDO**: O cabelo capacete e a assinatura visual da Janja. Ele deve ser absurdamente grande, desproporcional, e se mover como uma massa solida. Nunca desenhe cabelo normal.

2. **A boca EXAGERA**: A boca da Janja e a segunda piada visual. Ela fala mais que todo mundo, entao a boca deve ser grotescamente grande, especialmente em ataques e gritos.

3. **A bolsa e MAIOR que ela**: A bolsa de grife deve ser visivelmente desproporcional. Em nenhum momento a bolsa deve parecer um acessorio normal.

4. **O tapete E ela**: O tapete vermelho e uma extensao da personalidade. Ela nunca pisa no chao "normal". O tapete vai e volta, e atras dela sempre se desfaz em dinheiro.

5. **Unhas = garras**: As unhas nao sao longas, sao GARRAS. Vermelhas, curvadas, sempre agarrando algo (cartao, celular, ombro do Lula).

6. **Relacao com Lula**: Nos sprites de duo, a dinamica visual deve ser clara -- ela e a PUPPETEER, ele e o PUPPET. Ela e menor fisicamente mas DOMINA visualmente.

7. **Referencia G20**: O "FORA ELON MUSK!" e o momento mais icônico. Deve ter a MAXIMA energia visual -- grito, onda de choque, cabelo arrepiado, face vermelha.

8. **Paleta suja**: Nunca use cores limpas. Tudo deve ter um tom sujo, saturado, como se impresso em papel barato de revista underground.
