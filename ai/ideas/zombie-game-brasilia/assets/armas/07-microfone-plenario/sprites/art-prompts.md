# Microfone do Plenario — Art Generation Prompts

> Weapon sprites are 48x48 pixel art, projectiles are 32x32 pixel art. All with transparent background.
> Style anchor: Robert Crumb underground comix + Brazilian political satire + B-movie horror. Grotesque, exaggerated, dirty saturated colors. Thick outlines, heavy shadows. Sonic/electric visual effects.
