# Bigode da Justica -- Prompts para Geracao de Arte IA

## Visao Geral da Arma

O "Bigode da Justica" e a arma exclusiva do Eneas Carneiro (O Fantasma Patriota). E o bigode iconico do Eneas que se DESTACA do rosto e gira como boomerang, cortando inimigos. E tratado com a mesma REVERENCIA do personagem -- nunca comico, sempre epico.

## PROMPT 01: Sprite Sheet de Voo / Rotacao (8 frames, 32x32px)

### Prompt Principal
```
pixel art sprite sheet, 32x32 pixels per frame, 8 frames showing full 360-degree rotation,
underground comix art style inspired by Robert Crumb,

a LARGE THICK HANDLEBAR MUSTACHE spinning as a BOOMERANG WEAPON:
the mustache is dark brown (#3A2A1A) with thick black outlines (2px),
wide crescent/croissant shape approximately 26x10 pixels,
thick dense center (where it attaches to the face),
two long curving tips pointing upward that serve as BLADES,
the tips are sharp and gleam with golden light (#FFD700),

8 rotation frames showing the mustache spinning:
frame 1: 0 degrees (horizontal, tips pointing left and right)
frame 2: 45 degrees clockwise
frame 3: 90 degrees (vertical, tips pointing up and down)
frame 4: 135 degrees
frame 5: 180 degrees (horizontal inverted)
frame 6: 225 degrees
frame 7: 270 degrees (vertical inverted)
frame 8: 315 degrees (nearly completing full rotation)

golden aura glow surrounding the mustache (2px radius, #D4A017 at 30% opacity),
speed lines trailing behind (1px white at 40% opacity),
golden energy trail in an arc behind the spinning path,
small hair particle debris occasionally flying off,

the mustache should look DANGEROUS and SACRED simultaneously,
like a holy relic repurposed as a deadly weapon,
thick irregular outlines, cross-hatching texture on the hair strands,
transparent background (alpha channel),
horizontal sprite sheet layout
```

### Prompt de Refinamento
```
refine this sprite sheet to emphasize:
- each tip of the mustache GLEAMS with a golden flash (1px) as it rotates to the leading position
- the hair texture should have visible individual strands suggested by 1px dark lines
- motion blur on the tips (2px blur in the direction of rotation)
- the golden trail should form a CONTINUOUS ARC suggesting the boomerang flight path
- maintain thick 2px outlines on all frames
- the center of the mustache (attachment point) should have a subtle golden glow
- the overall impression is "this mustache could CUT STEEL with dignity"
```

## Guia de Uso dos Prompts

### Para Midjourney
- Adicionar: `--ar 1:1 --s 200 --q 2 --v 6` para frames individuais
- Para sprite sheets: `--ar 8:1` (voo 8 frames) ou `--ar 4:1` (4 frames)
- Usar: `--no gradient, smooth, 3D, realistic, clean`

### Para DALL-E 3
- Prefixar: `Create a pixel art image. `
- Ser explicito: `The image must be exactly 32x32 pixels.`
- Adicionar: `Use hard pixel edges, no anti-aliasing, no smooth gradients.`

### Para Stable Diffusion
- Modelo: `pixel-art-xl` ou `retro-pixel-art`
- Negative: `smooth, gradient, 3D, realistic, blurry, antialiased, soft, modern`
- Sampler: `DPM++ 2M Karras`, Steps: 30, CFG: 8

### Para Aseprite (Recomendado)
- Usar prompts de IA para gerar CONCEITO visual
- Redesenhar manualmente no Aseprite com as especificacoes exatas do sprite-spec.md
- METODO IDEAL para manter consistencia com os outros assets do jogo

## Checklist de Qualidade

Antes de aprovar qualquer asset do Bigode da Justica:

- [ ] A forma do bigode e IDENTICA ao bigode no rosto do Eneas?
- [ ] As pontas parecem AFIADAS quando em voo?
- [ ] A aura dourada esta presente em TODOS os frames?
- [ ] A rotacao e FLUIDA (8 posicoes cobrindo 360 graus)?
- [ ] Outlines grossos e irregulares (2px, estilo Crumb)?
- [ ] Textura de pelos sugerida por linhas finas?
- [ ] Onomatopeia "SHLICK!" (nao THWACK) no impacto?
- [ ] Trail dourado visivel durante o voo?
- [ ] Flash de re-acoplamento e SATISFATORIO?
- [ ] O tom geral e de REVERENCIA EPICA?
- [ ] Cross-hatching nas sombras?
- [ ] Alpha channels corretos?
