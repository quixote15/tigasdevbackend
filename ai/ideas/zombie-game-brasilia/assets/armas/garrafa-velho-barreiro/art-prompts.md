# Art Prompts — Garrafa de Velho Barreiro Incendiaria

## Estilo Geral
Todos os prompts seguem o estilo Andre Guedes: grotesco, linhas grossas irregulares, cores saturadas-sujas, humor negro. A garrafa e FEIA, suja, velha — nao e uma garrafa bonita de bar chique.

---

## Prompt Base (copiar em TODOS os prompts)

**Positive base**: `2D game sprite, top-down perspective, pixel art style 64x64, Brazilian underground comix style, thick irregular outlines, dirty saturated colors, grotesque caricature style inspired by Robert Crumb and Brazilian political satire, transparent background, game asset`

**Negative base**: `3D render, photorealistic, clean lines, smooth shading, beautiful, elegant, refined, cute, anime style, chibi, western cartoon, Pixar style, Disney style, symmetrical, perfect proportions, HDR, bloom, lens flare`
