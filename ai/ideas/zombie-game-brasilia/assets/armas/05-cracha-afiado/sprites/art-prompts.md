# Cracha Afiado — Art Generation Prompts

> All prompts target a 32x32 pixel art sprite with transparent background.
> Style anchor: Robert Crumb underground comix + Brazilian political satire + B-movie horror. Grotesque, exaggerated, dirty saturated colors. Thick outlines, heavy shadows.
