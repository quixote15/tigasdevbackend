## Post-Processing Notes
- Weapon/projectile frames: clean to exact 32x32 pixel grid
- Explosion frames: clean to exact 48x48 pixel grid
- The politician caricature must be GENERIC -- no specific real person
- Emphasize the grotesque fake smile and white teeth on the face
- The burning fuse is the key detail that transforms pamphlet into weapon
- Confetti colors should be saturated campaign colors -- garish and festive
- "BOOM!" should feel chunkier and blockier than other weapons' onomatopeias
- Politician face fragments in the explosion are critical for the dark humor
- The scorch mark in final frames should work as a ground decal texture
- Frisbee spin cycle (frames 4-7) must clearly show front/edge/back/edge rotation
- Campaign aesthetic: cheap, mass-produced, garish, disposable, explosive
- Assemble weapon sheet: 320x32 (10 frames x 32px)
- Assemble explosion sheet: 288x48 (6 frames x 48px)
