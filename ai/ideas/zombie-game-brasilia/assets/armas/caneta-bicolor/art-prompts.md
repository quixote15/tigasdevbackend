# Art Prompts — Caneta Bicolor (Arma Exclusiva do BolsoLula)

## Estilo Geral
Estilo Andre Guedes: grotesco, linhas grossas irregulares, cores saturadas-sujas. A caneta e GIGANTE (tamanho de bastao de baseball), plastico barato rachado, mordida, com corpo bicolor — metade vermelha (Lula/PT), metade verde-amarela (Bolsonaro). Nao e elegante. E uma BIC de escritorio de politico, so que monstruosa.

---

## Prompt Base

**Positive base**: `2D game sprite, top-down perspective, pixel art style, Brazilian underground comix style, thick irregular outlines, dirty saturated colors, grotesque caricature style inspired by Robert Crumb and Brazilian political satire, transparent background, game asset`

**Negative base**: `3D render, photorealistic, clean lines, smooth shading, beautiful, elegant, refined, cute, anime style, fountain pen, calligraphy pen, luxury pen, Montblanc, Pixar, Disney`
