# Art Prompts — Arma Brochavel (Revolver Dourado do Bolsonaro)

## Estilo Geral
Estilo Andre Guedes: grotesco, linhas grossas irregulares, cores saturadas-sujas. O revolver e CAFONA — dourado de camelo, nao de joalheria. E ridiculo, exagerado, com adesivo da bandeira torto.

---

## Prompt Base

**Positive base**: `2D game sprite, top-down perspective, pixel art style, Brazilian underground comix style, thick irregular outlines, dirty saturated colors, grotesque caricature style inspired by Robert Crumb and Brazilian political satire, transparent background, game asset`

**Negative base**: `3D render, photorealistic, clean lines, smooth shading, beautiful, elegant, refined, cute, anime style, realistic gun, military realistic, tactical, modern firearm, Pixar, Disney`

---

## Sprite: Arma Em Mao

### Frame 1 — Idle postura machao
```
2D game sprite, top-down perspective, pixel art style 32x32, Brazilian underground comix style, thick irregular outlines, dirty saturated colors, grotesque, transparent background,
a ridiculously oversized golden cowboy revolver held in two hands, the gold is TACKY and CHEAP looking (#DAA520), not elegant gold, gaudy shiny almost plastic-looking golden surface with excessive lens flare spots, the barrel is comically too long (compensation joke), a crooked Brazilian flag sticker (#009739, #FFDF00) stuck sloppily on the grip, visible cylinder with 6 bullets some rusty (#B87333), a green-yellow ribbon tied to the trigger guard, greasy fingerprints on the gold surface
```
**Negative**: `3D, photorealistic, elegant gun, military weapon, modern gun, matte black, tactical, real gold, refined, anime, Beretta, Glock, assault rifle`

### Frame 2 — Idle com gesto de arminha
```
2D game sprite, top-down perspective, pixel art style 32x32, Brazilian underground comix style, thick irregular outlines, grotesque, transparent background,
same tacky golden revolver, plus a hand making a "finger gun" gesture next to it (iconic Brazilian right-wing gesture), ridiculous lens flare on the gold barrel, the "finger gun" hand is pudgy with short fingers, both the real gun and finger gun pointing in the same direction, comedic juxtaposition
```
**Negative**: `3D, photorealistic, elegant, military, modern, tactical, real gold, anime, cool-looking`

### Frame 3 — Giro no dedo (quase derruba)
```
2D game sprite, top-down perspective, pixel art style 32x32, Brazilian underground comix style, thick irregular outlines, grotesque, transparent background,
tacky golden revolver spinning on a pudgy finger like a cowboy trick, the gun is mid-spin at 45 degrees, WOBBLING dangerously as if about to fly off the finger, motion lines showing unstable spin, the finger is sweating, comedic moment of near-disaster, the Brazilian flag sticker is peeling off from centrifugal force
```
**Negative**: `3D, photorealistic, smooth spin, cool cowboy, elegant, professional gunslinger, anime`
